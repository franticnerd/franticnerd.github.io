Learning Objective

This course will provide an overview of the latest machine learning techniques for Natural Language Processing (NLP), with a strong focus on deep learning methods that have seen tremendous success recently. The students will be introduced to current NLP challenges, key solutions and their strengths and weaknesses.

The goal of this bootcamp is for students to gain a comprehensive understanding of NLP problems and be able to choose appropriate statistical models, as well as develop innovative solutions to tackle ongoing research challenges in the field. This program will benefit students interested in solving real-world text data problems, as well as those pursuing cutting-edge research in text mining, NLP and artificial intelligence.
