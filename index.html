Chao Zhang

Ph.D. in CS

UIUC

I am an incoming assistant professor at School of Computational Science and Engineering, Georgia Tech. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from UIUC under the advisement of Prof. Jiawei Han.

My research interests fall in the area of data mining and machine learning. My current work focuses on developing label-efficient and robust methods that turn massive data (e.g., text, spatiotemporal data) into knowledge for task support and decision making.

I am actively looking for talented and motivated students to work with me. Please email me if you are interested.
